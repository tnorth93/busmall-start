Instructions

Focus group member, thank you for participating in our market research study. You will be shown a series of 3 images of products that you will compare with one another. Please click the image of the product that you would be most likely to purchase. Once you have voted on 25 sets of images, an alert will prompt you to seek out the nearest Market Research Team Member for debriefing. If you need assistance completing the survey, feel free to ask any of the proctors in the survey room at any time. Please do not speak with any of the other survey participants in the room or leave the testing area at any time.
